What is H21?

H21 is a daily 21 Questions game powered by AI. A secret subject is chosen each day for every topic and difficulty combination. Your goal is to identify the subject by asking up to 21 yes-or-no questions.

How to Play

  1. Pick a topic and difficulty on the home page. Each combination has its own daily puzzle—you can play multiple puzzles per day by choosing different topics or difficulties.
  2. Ask yes/no questions to narrow down the secret subject. Type your question and press Ask. You can also make a direct guess at any time.
  3. Read the AI's response. Each answer is one of:
    • yes — clearly affirmative
    • no — clearly negative
    • partially — partly correct or context-dependent
    • depends — interpretation-dependent
    • win — you guessed it!
    After the game ends, you can click why? next to any answer to see the AI's reasoning.
  4. Win or lose. If you identify the subject within 21 questions you win. Otherwise the solution is revealed.
  5. Share your result using the share button after a game ends. It copies a spoiler-free emoji summary to your clipboard.

Topics

The home page lists available topics. You can also request new topics using the form at the bottom of the home page. Each topic gets its own independent daily puzzle per difficulty level.

Difficulty Levels

  • Easy — well-known subjects that most people would recognize.
  • Medium — subjects that a knowledgeable person would know.
  • Hard — obscure subjects that require specialist knowledge.

Daily Puzzles

Each topic + difficulty combination generates one puzzle per day. Everyone playing the same combination on the same day gets the same secret subject, so you can compare results with friends.

Share Emoji Legend

When you share your results, each question is represented by an emoji:

  • 🟢 yes
  • 🔴 no
  • 🟡 partially
  • 🔵 depends
  • 🏆 win

Tips

  • Start broad: ask about the era, region, or field before narrowing down.
  • Use the partially and depends answers as clues—they often hint that you're on the right track but need to be more specific.
  • You can ask your question in any language; the AI will always respond in English.
  • Questions are limited to 500 characters.
